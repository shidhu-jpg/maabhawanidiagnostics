Our Story

A DECADE OF DELIVERING CERTAINTY THROUGH DATA AND DESIGN.

Founded by a collective of radiologists and architects, Clinical Precision was born from a singular realization: The environment of care is as critical as the care itself.

We spent three years auditing traditional diagnostic workflows, identifying the points of friction that cause patient anxiety and clinical error. Our first facility in 2012 was a prototype for a new kind of medical sanctuary—minimalist, silent, and powered by the world's most advanced imaging arrays.

Today, we serve over 200,000 patients annually across our boutique network of centers. We remain focused on the "boutique" scale, ensuring that every patient receives a tailored, non-industrialized experience that prioritizes their dignity alongside their data.
